## About Connaisseur

A robust Kubernetes admission controller that enforces cryptographic verification and trust anchoring of container
images throughout your cluster infrastructure.

Connaisseur delivers comprehensive supply chain attestation and systematic image provenance validation for containerized
workloads in Kubernetes environments. The solution operates by intercepting pod creation and update requests at the API
layer, performing automated image discovery and cryptographic signature validation against configured authorization
policies. Upon validation completion, the controller enforces granular admission decisions to maintain image integrity
and compliance posture across your cluster.

## About Docker Hardened Images

Docker Hardened Images are built to meet the highest security and compliance standards. They provide a trusted
foundation for containerized workloads by incorporating security best practices from the start.

### Why use Docker Hardened Images?

These images are published with near-zero known CVEs, include signed provenance, and come with a complete Software Bill
of Materials (SBOM) and VEX metadata. They're designed to secure your software supply chain while fitting seamlessly
into existing Docker workflows.
