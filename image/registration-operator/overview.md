## About registration-operator

The registration-operator is a component of the
[Open Cluster Management (OCM)](https://github.com/open-cluster-management-io/ocm) project, a community-driven
initiative for multi-cluster management on Kubernetes.

The registration-operator provides two operators:

- **Cluster Manager** — installs OCM foundational components on the hub cluster, including the registration controller,
  work controller, placement controller, and add-on manager.
- **Klusterlet** — installs agent components on managed clusters to enable registration, work execution, and add-on
  management.

For more details, see https://open-cluster-management.io/.
