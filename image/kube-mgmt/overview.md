## About kube-mgmt

kube-mgmt is a sidecar for managing Open Policy Agent (OPA) instances in Kubernetes. It automatically discovers and
loads policies and data from Kubernetes resources such as ConfigMaps and Secrets into OPA, enabling centralized policy
management across your cluster.

kube-mgmt simplifies OPA deployments by handling the synchronization of policies and data, so you can focus on writing
policies rather than managing how they're distributed.

For more information, visit the official repository: https://github.com/open-policy-agent/kube-mgmt
