## About Kafka Exporter

Kafka Exporter is a Prometheus exporter for Kafka metrics including information about brokers, topics, consumer groups
and lag information amongst others. For other metrics from Kafka, have a look at the Docker Hardened JMX exporter image.

For more details, visit https://github.com/danielqsj/kafka_exporter.
