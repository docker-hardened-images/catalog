## About Bash

Bash is the GNU⁠ Project's Bourne Again SHell, a complete implementation of the
[IEEE POSIX and Open Group shell specification](http://www.opengroup.org/onlinepubs/9699919799/nfindex.html)⁠ with
interactive command line editing, job control on architectures that support it, csh-like features such as history
substitution and brace expansion, and a slew of other features.

For more details, visit http://www.case.edu/php/chet/bash/bashtop.html.
