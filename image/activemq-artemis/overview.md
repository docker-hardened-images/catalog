## About Apache ActiveMQ Artemis

Apache ActiveMQ Artemis is a high-performance, open-source message broker designed for modern, scalable, and
multi-protocol messaging systems. As the next-generation successor to ActiveMQ Classic, Artemis offers advanced features
and improved performance, making it suitable for a wide range of messaging scenarios.

For more details, visit https://activemq.apache.org/components/artemis/documentation/.

## About Docker Hardened Images

Docker Hardened Images are built to meet the highest security and compliance standards. They provide a trusted

These images are published with zero-known CVEs, include signed provenance, and come with a complete Software Bill of
Materials (SBOM) and VEX metadata. They're designed to secure your software supply chain while fitting seamlessly into
existing Docker workflows.
